# Conversion Copy Upgrades

## Global Error Messages

These standardized microcopy upgrades replace technical jargon with human-readable, empathetic, and actionable guidance. Apply universally.

- **401 Unauthorized**: "Please sign in to continue"
- **403 Forbidden**: "You don't have permission to do this"
- **404 Not Found**: "We couldn't find what you're looking for"
- **500 Internal Server Error**: "Something went wrong on our end — please try again in a moment"
- **Network Error**: "Check your connection and try again"
- **Out of Stock**: "Unavailable right now. Save it and we'll notify you when it's back."
- **Payment Failed**: "Payment wasn't processed. No amount was charged. Please try again."

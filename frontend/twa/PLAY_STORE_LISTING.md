## Full Description (4000 chars max)

CMGROUPS brings technology, services, and education together in one powerful app.

🛒 SHOP
Browse a curated selection of computers, laptops, components, and accessories. Compare products side-by-side, read reviews, add to your wishlist, and check out securely — all from your phone.

🔧 SERVICES
Need computer repair? Book a technician in minutes. Choose from repair, AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract), and installation services with real-time slot booking. Track your service from booking to completion with OTP-verified service delivery.

📚 LEARN
Enroll in professional courses including Tally ERP, computer fundamentals, and more. Track your progress, access course materials, and earn certificates — all within the app.

✨ KEY FEATURES
• Product comparison with detailed specs
• Secure checkout with order tracking
• Push notifications for order updates and service reminders
• Referral program — invite friends and earn rewards
• Offline browsing — view products even without internet
• Fast and lightweight — installs in seconds

🔒 TRUST & SECURITY
• Secure authentication
• Verified service technicians
• Encrypted transactions
• Transparent pricing with no hidden charges

Built with care for the Indian market. Available in English.
